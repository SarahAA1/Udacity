Conclusions¶

This was an interesting dataset.

  • The average movie duration is 109 minutes.
  • There is a significant decrease at the movie duration from year to year.
  • There is a moderate correlation between revenue and budget. That's mean if the movie has high budget could has a high revenue as well.
  • The highest number of movies was in 2011 with 200 movies.

Limitation¶

  • Removing missing or Null values from revenue and budget columns might skew our analysis and could show unintended bias towards in our analysis.
  • There is no any information about which the currency is used in this dataset nd our analysis is limited to the numerical values of revenue and budget
